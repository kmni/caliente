About CCR

Candace and Dave Berry of Caliente Creek Ranch began breeding Arabians almost two decades ago, but noticed in the latter part of 2011 that Hunter Pleasure and English pleasure horses bred for Amateurs were in short supply and large demand.

Our herd is now made of primarily Arabian Hunter Pleasure and English Pleasure horses with the following qualifications.

  • extremely well bred
  • of excellent conformation
  • have well balanced motion, front and rear

We are very proud of our history of breeding National Champions and are excited to show you our special Hunter and English horses. Keep up with us on Facebook, we would like to be your friend!
